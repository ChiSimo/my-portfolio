I am Simona Chiapperino, I turn ideas into codeand stories into design,

I love to explore and create, with a real passion for design, storytelling, and tech. Every big or small idea should be brought to life through thoughtful visuals and practical design. My path started with drawing and painting, and grew into working with digital tools and programming. I mix creative thinking with problem-solving in web development to build websites and apps that grab people's attention. From planning how users navigate a site to writing clean, structured code, I put care and thought into every project. What I'm after is pretty straightforward: I want to turn ideas into simple designs that are easy for everyone to use and focused on what users need.
